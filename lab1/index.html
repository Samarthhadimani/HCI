Instructions for Animal Sequence Experiment

1. On the next screen,you will be shown some list of Animals.

2. Try to remember the sets visually and mentally.

3. You will have only 8 seconds to look at the Animal Names and remember them,after which the screen will automatically move to the next task.

4. A timer as shown on the right will indicate the time elapsed.

5. When you are ready, click on the START button.

6. Once clicked,you will not be able to return to this page till the experiment is over.
